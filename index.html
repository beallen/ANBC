2018 Monitoring Program

In 2018, the Alberta Native Bee Council was able to organize a group of volunteers across Alberta to sample Alberta's native bee populations. By using this information, in addition to open source data sets, we decribe the current distribution, and the impacts of land-use change on native bee abundance and diversity.
